About Vignan University

Vignan's Foundation for Science, Technology & Research is a Deemed university in the Guntur district in Andhra Pradesh, India. It is in the rural area of Vadlamudi, in the southeast of Guntur City and the northeast of Tenali City.The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) ranked it 75 among Universities in 2023.

Vision

To evolve into a centre of excellence in Science & Technology through creative and innovative practices in teaching - learning, towards promoting academic achievement and research excellence to produce internationally accepted, competitive and world class professionals who are psychologically strong & emotionally balanced imbued with social consciousness & ethical values.

Mission

To provide high quality academic programmes, training activities, research facilities and opportunities supported by continuous industry - institute interaction aimed at promoting employability, entrepreneurship, leadership and research aptitude among students and contribute to the economic and technological development of the region, state and nation.

Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) course was started in the year 1997 in this institution.Department of CSE offers the following UG and PG programs.

Information Technology

Information Technology is the soul and psyche of many engineering branches. These fields are instrumental in bringing the world to where it is today. B.Tech in Information technology course was started in the year 1999, MCA course in 2006, Ph.D in 2013 and BCA in 2015.

Electrical & Electronical Engineering

The Department of Electrical Engineering, one of the nine founding Departments of Vignan's Foundation for Science, Technology & Research (Deemed to be University), is started in the year 2009 by adopting the motto: Excellence in Engineering Education.

Mechanical Engineering

VFSTR offers Mechanical Engineering programs at bachelor, master and PhD levels. Our strong and interactive curriculum incorporates learning with hands-on practice that makes the students capable of taking their career to the next level.Right from its inception in 1997, the Department has been focusing on quality teaching with integrated laboratory practice..

Our Facilities

Best Cafetaria

A well furnished and spacious cafeteria worth of Rs 1.5 Crore, with a 400 seating capacity is located in the heart of the campus. It provides all types of nutritious and hygienic eatables and beverages to cater to the multi-ethnic tastes of the students, staff and visitors at reasonable prices. Apart from the college canteen, there are several food kiosks providing snacks and refreshments operating in and around the academic blocks on the campus. This Canteen is open on all working days, and also during additional working hours

World Class Library

Central Library is an air conditioned, aesthetically designed facility with a built up area of 5902 Sqm surrounded by plenty of indoor greenery, modern furniture and reprographic conveniences.The library subscribes to host of on line IEEE, ASME, ASCE, Springer, JSTOR & EBSCO journals which fosters research zeal, among both the faculty & students.Multimedia systems have been installed in the library to enable learning through digital resources like CDs, Internet etc.More than 6003 multimedia CDs are made available for easy reference.

Largest Ground

The campus is equipped with facilities for all outdoor games like cricket, football, volleyball, tennis, basketball, and a 400-meter track athletics. An Indoor Sports Complex offers provision for indoor games like table tennis, badminton etc.To keep the students motivated,the physical educational department under the supervision of a well qualified Physical Director conducts intramurals round the year for keeping the sports persons well practiced and ready to participate whenever, there is a state level or national level event.
